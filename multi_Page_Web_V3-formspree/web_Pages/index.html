About Me

I was six years old when I first picked up a golf club, borrowing my brother’s during one of his lessons at West Middlesex Golf Club. On my very first strike, the ball soared 45 yards, flying over my Dad’s and coach’s heads, immediately drawing all attention to me.

By the age of eleven, I had already driven 311 yards off the sixth hole at West Middlesex, showcasing a natural aptitude for distance and precision. I quickly developed a passion for competitive golf, travelling across the UK and US to participate in junior tournaments. My early successes included victory at the US Kids Golf European Championships with a score of 12 under par and winning the Middlesex Ladies Championship at just fourteen years old.

Most recently, I earned a place on the Wales National Squad and will be competing for selection in 2026. At the conclusion of the season, I will begin my US collegiate career on a scholarship at Seminole State College, continuing to develop my game at an elite level.
