About Me

My first experience in golf was picking up my brother's club whilst he was receiving a lesson at West Middlesex Golf Club. I struck the ball, and it flew high over my Dad's and coach's heads for about 45 yards. They turned in astonishment and focused on me instead of my brother. Even at age 6, I had a surprising ability to hit long distances. By 11, I hit a 311-yard drive off hole 6 at West Middlesex CG. I loved performing in competitions across the UK and US. I 1st many junior competitions, including the US Kids Golf European Championships with 12 under par, and the Middlesex Ladies Championship at age 14. I recently earned a place with the Wales National Squad and will compete for selection in 2026. At the end of the season, I will begin my US scholarship at Seminole State College.
